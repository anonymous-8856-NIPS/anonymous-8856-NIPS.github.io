Core narrative

From Oracle Fragments to a Continuous Manifold

The project page is organized as the paper is argued: first the failure mode, then the data, the method, the inference engine, and finally the mechanistic evidence.

Oracle Bone decipherment is not merely a visual retrieval task. Many characters shift sharply across intermediate dynasties, and some glyphs disappear before reaching later scripts. MSEF reframes the problem as temporally coherent motion: each glyph becomes an era-specific point, inter-era transitions are learned as continuous flows, and modern candidates are accepted only when they remain consistent when traced backward.

  1. 01Single-period matching is brittle. A later-period glyph may look dissimilar even when it belongs to the same historical trajectory.
  2. 02Pre-exploratory diagnosis motivates the method. The paper first examines why generative, retrieval, and rule-based paradigms fail under missing intermediate evidence and combinatorial variation.
  3. 03MSEF models evolution continuously. Neural ODE dynamics connect era-specific manifolds, allowing incomplete cross-era pairs to supervise a shared evolution space.
  4. 04CBED performs survival-aware inference. It recalls candidates forward, prunes implausible paths, and verifies the final candidate backward through time.
  5. 05Post-analysis tests whether the model learned structure. Mechanistic visualizations inspect attention, saliency, topology, and survival pressure rather than treating the model as a black box.

Dataset

Fine-Grained Cross-Era Evidence

The dataset section explains why the method is trainable: complete evolution chains are limited, but partial correspondences across periods provide abundant supervision.

The dataset is not a supporting detail; it is part of the method. MSEF relies on cross-era pairs that do not require a full five-stage chain for every character. This lets the model learn a shared continuous trajectory from incomplete historical evidence.

  1. 01Cross-era coverage. The visual story spans Oracle Bone, Bronze, Seal, Clerical, and Regular scripts rather than treating one period as the only target.
  2. 02Fine-grained time. Subperiod and regional information preserve variation instead of collapsing all historical forms into coarse labels.
  3. 03Partial chains as signal. Non-complete pairs become useful training evidence under a continuous manifold formulation.

Method

Pre → MSEF → CBED → Post: How the Method Reads the Past

This section keeps the method readable: diagnose the problem, model evolution as flow, verify candidates bidirectionally, and inspect the learned mechanisms.

01

Encode OBI Query

Extract visual, structural, semantic, contextual, and spatiotemporal features.

02

Forward Recall

Move ancient glyph coordinates forward through era-conditioned manifolds.

03

Survival Gate

Suppress paths that likely became extinct or historically implausible.

04

Backward Verification

Trace modern candidates back and prune those inconsistent with the OBI query.

05

Mechanistic Audit

Use topology, attention, saliency, and patching analyses to test what the model learned.
